Little Lemon is a family-owned restaurant that offers a unique dining experience with delicious food and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients to create flavorful dishes that cater to all tastes. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, Little Lemon is the perfect place to enjoy a meal with family and friends.

Established in 2005, Little Lemon has been serving the community with passion and dedication. Our mission is to provide high-quality food and exceptional service to our customers. Over the years, we have grown from a small eatery to a well-known restaurant that is loved by locals and visitors alike.

Our team is made up of talented chefs, friendly waitstaff, and dedicated managers who work together to ensure that every customer has a memorable dining experience. We believe in creating a positive work environment where everyone feels valued and motivated to deliver their best.
